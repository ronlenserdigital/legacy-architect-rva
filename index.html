About Craig

Locally-owned.
Human-led.

Forrest Dane Cauthorn wasn't just a name. He was Craig's best friend. The guy from that first job at TGI Fridays. The one who always had his back. In 2006, Dane was killed in Iraq.

In the middle of grief, Craig and Dane's father were drowning in details. Accounts to track down. A truck to get home. Paperwork that doesn't wait for anyone. They had love and support, but they didn't have clarity.

That experience never left.

Over 15 years in insurance, hospice, and hospital operations, Craig saw the same pattern again and again. Not because families didn't care, but because no one had helped them build the right kind of document.

"You don't do this for yourself. You do it for them, because no one should have to figure it all out in the dark." Craig Rothchild
